<h1> Different Types of Exercise</h1>
<br>
<h2> Aerobic exercise</h2>
<p> Aerobic exercise is one of the most important types of exercise. <br>The word aerobic, by definition, means that
    oxygen is present in the process. <br> While all types of exercise of course include oxygen, aerobic exercise
    focuses the
    fitness of one's <em>lungs and heart </em>. <br></p>
<h3> Aerobic exercises include:</h3>
<ul>
    <li> Swimming</li>
    <li> Biking</li>
    <li> Running</li>
    <li> Playing sports</li>
</ul>

<h2> Strength training</h2>
<p> Strength training is what most poeple think of when someone says that they go to a gym. <br>
    Strength training is the act of doing bodyweight exercises of lifting weights in order to break down a muscle. <br>
    After the muscle is broken down, it will build back stronger and larger. <br> Some people do strength training just
    to look better, but other do it to become more athletic overall.</p>

<h2> Stretching</h2>
<p> Almost everyone knows what stretching is, but many people may not know that it is actually a type of exercise! <br>
    This is because stretching is the act of putting muscles in a position of tension to loosten them and be able to
    move
    them more freely. <br> Stretching is a very important part of physical fitness that many people skip out on,
    resulting
    in injuries like muscle strains or tears. <br></p>
