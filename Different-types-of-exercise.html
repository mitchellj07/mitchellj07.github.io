Different Types of Exercise


Aerobic exercise

Aerobic exercise is one of the most important types of exercise.
The word aerobic, by definition, means that oxygen is present in the process.
While all types of exercise of course include oxygen, aerobic exercise focuses the fitness of one's lungs and heart .

Aerobic exercises include:

  • Swimming
  • Biking
  • Running
  • Playing sports

Strength training

Strength training is what most poeple think of when someone says that they go to a gym.
Strength training is the act of doing bodyweight exercises of lifting weights in order to break down a muscle.
After the muscle is broken down, it will build back stronger and larger.
Some people do strength training just to look better, but other do it to become more athletic overall.

Stretching

Almost everyone knows what stretching is, but many people may not know that it is actually a type of exercise!
This is because stretching is the act of putting muscles in a position of tension to loosten them and be able to move them more freely.
Stretching is a very important part of physical fitness that many people skip out on, resulting in injuries like muscle strains or tears.

Balance exercises

Balance exercises are a type of exercise that is often overlooked.
Balance exercises like yoga poses allow people to have more control of their body and live an all around more comfortable life.
